Iperbot

IperBot is an internal project developed by several interns and designers at Iperdesign. This was my first project in Unity. It was a deep dive into a very unfamiliar API which made for a challenging experience. Testing the game with users was a major part of this project as well. It was important that the difficulty was appropriate in such a way that experienced gamers could have fun and less experienced gamers would not be overwhelmed.

Valve Visaid

​ The visual aid is a simple app to help customers understand their valve device. The app was designed with engaging UI interactions to direct users through the graphics and copy in a way that they could efficiently grasp the information. My role was creating the UI through XCode to copy the designed UI as closely as possible. This project particularly improved my knowledge of constraints in creating a layout that could adapt to different sized devices.

Government Bodies

​ Government Bodies is a single view app that allows users to mix their favorite animals with their favorite (or least favorite) political figures. This was my first app and a very fun project. Users can scroll or randomize through images and send their creations to friends.

Make-A-Note

​ Make A Note is a note-taking app for iOS, created on XCode, allowing users to write notes and save them to their devices. I developed this app in my free time. It was very helpful in learning how to handle multiple view controllers. It is available for download on the Apple app store.
